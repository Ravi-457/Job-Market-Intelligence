# India Job Market Intelligence Dashboard (2024–2026)

## Project Overview

The India Job Market Intelligence Dashboard is a data analytics project developed using SQL and Power BI to analyze hiring trends, salary insights, company ratings, job openings, applicant demand, and workforce distribution across various industries in India.

The dashboard helps identify the most active hiring sectors, highest-paying job roles, preferred work modes, and experience-level demand in the Indian job market.

## Objectives

* Analyze job market trends across industries.
* Identify the highest-paying job roles.
* Understand hiring demand by experience level.
* Compare work mode preferences (Remote, Hybrid, On-site).
* Evaluate company ratings and applicant competition.
* Provide interactive insights through Power BI visualizations.
